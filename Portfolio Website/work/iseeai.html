1. 👁️iSeeAI: Empowering the Visually Impaired with Artificial Intelligence

Duration : Aug 2023 - Present

Excited to introduce iSeeAI, my final year project—a groundbreaking AI Assistance Application dedicated to empowering visually impaired individuals. 🌐🤖

Overview:
iSeeAI is a comprehensive AI-powered solution designed to enhance the daily lives of the visually impaired. It combines cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), data science (DS), and machine learning (ML) to provide a multifunctional tool addressing various challenges faced by individuals with visual impairments.

Key Functionalities:
📍 Navigation Assistance : iSeeAI offers real-time navigation support, providing users with voice-guided directions and obstacle detection to navigate their surroundings confidently.
💊 Medicine Recognition : Leveraging image recognition algorithms, the application identifies and announces information about medicines, promoting safety and independence.
🔍 Object Recognition : The AI-driven system recognizes and describes everyday objects, facilitating improved interaction with the environment.

How It Works:
📷 Computer Vision: iSeeAI utilizes computer vision to process live camera feed, enabling real-time identification of objects, medicines, and obstacles. 🤖 Machine Learning: The application employs machine learning algorithms for continuous improvement, adapting to users' unique needs and preferences. 🗣️ Natural Language Processing (NLP): NLP is integrated to communicate information in a clear and user-friendly manner, enhancing overall user experience.

Benefits:
✨ Promoting Independence: iSeeAI empowers visually impaired individuals to independently navigate their surroundings, identify objects, and manage medications.
🌐 Accessible Technology: Ensuring inclusivity, the application makes cutting-edge technology accessible to everyone, regardless of visual abilities.
🤝 Community Support: iSeeAI fosters a sense of community by connecting users, facilitating shared experiences, and providing a platform for knowledge exchange.
